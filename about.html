Our Story

No Middleman, A Fair Price, From the Heart of Egypt's Land

Waraqat Shajar is a digital platform connecting Egyptian farmers directly with traders, factories, and consumers — no brokers, no hidden commissions.

Why We Built Waraqat Shajar

For decades, Egyptian farmers received the lowest possible price for their harvest, while consumers paid many times more — with the difference absorbed by a long chain of middlemen who had no real connection to the crop. Waraqat Shajar was built to close that gap: one simple platform where a farmer can list their harvest for the whole world to see, and any trader, factory, or even end consumer can reach them directly and agree on a fair price with complete transparency — without anyone taking a cut of work they never did.

How the Platform Works

Four simple steps, from signing up to receiving your harvest

1
Create Your Account
Choose your account type — farmer, trader, factory, or consumer — and sign up in minutes.
2
List or Browse
Farmers list their harvest with their own price and quantity; everyone else browses the available categories.
3
Negotiate Directly
Direct in-app conversation, with a formal offer system that makes agreeing on price and quantity easy.
4
Agree & Deliver
Once you reach an agreement, complete the deal directly between yourselves, with full trust and transparency.
